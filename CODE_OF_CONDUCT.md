# Community Code of Conduct

**Version 1.0**

The Heildamm project is committed to fostering an inclusive, respectful, and professional community. This Code of Conduct establishes expectations for all members participating in the project and its associated spaces, including but not limited to repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, and communication channels.

---

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all participants, regardless of background, identity, experience, or other characteristics. We pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

## Expected Behavior

### Professional Conduct

- Conduct yourself professionally and respectfully in all interactions
- Use clear, constructive language in comments, discussions, and feedback
- Listen actively to others and consider diverse perspectives
- Be open to receiving and providing constructive criticism

### Inclusive Environment

- Welcome and support new contributors regardless of background
- Use inclusive language and avoid assumptions
- Respect different working styles and communication preferences
- Acknowledge and value different perspectives and expertise areas

### Collaborative Spirit

- Work toward shared goals and project improvement
- Share knowledge and help others learn
- Give credit where it is due
- Assume good intentions in others' communications

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

### Harassment and Discrimination

- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, race, ethnicity, religion, disability, age, or other protected characteristics
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Sexual harassment or unwelcome sexual advances
- Sustained disruption of discussion or events
- Targeted personal attacks or mockery

### Harmful Conduct

- Doxing or sharing private information without consent
- Trolling or intentionally inflammatory behavior
- Spam or off-topic content in project spaces
- Attempts to undermine project integrity or security
- Any form of harassment or bullying

### Professional Violations

- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of authorship
- Abusive communication or excessive negativity
- Attempts to manipulate or coerce other participants
- Conflicts of interest not properly disclosed

---

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct:

### Reporting Process

1. **Document the Incident**
   - Note the date, time, and nature of the violation
   - Collect relevant context or evidence
   - Identify any witnesses

2. **Report to Maintainers**
   - Email maintainers at [contact email to be added]
   - Or contact a project maintainer directly
   - Provide as much detail as possible

3. **Confidentiality**
   - Reports will be handled confidentially
   - Information will be shared only with necessary parties
   - Reporters' identities will be protected when possible

### Investigation and Resolution

- Reported violations will be investigated promptly
- The investigation process will be fair and thorough
- Appropriate action will be taken based on findings
- Parties involved will be notified of outcomes

---

## Enforcement

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- Warning or request for behavior modification
- Temporary suspension from project participation
- Permanent removal from the project and community
- Escalation to appropriate authorities if necessary

The severity of consequences will be proportionate to the violation and consider context and history.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project repositories and code
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- Project discussions and forums
- Project-related communication channels
- Project events (virtual or in-person)
- Any space representing the Heildamm project

---

## Acknowledgment

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant and other community guidelines. We appreciate the work of communities that have established guidelines that inspire and inform our own.

---

## Questions?

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct or need clarification on expected behavior, please contact the project maintainers.

**We are committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and professional community. Thank you for being part of Heildamm.**
